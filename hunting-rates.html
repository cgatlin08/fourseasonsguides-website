Dove Hunting — Day Hunts

Oklahoma & Texas hunts available

$150 per gun

Seven days a week. Book in advance.

All hunting should be booked in advance. We suggest a 2–3 day hunt, but a 1-day hunt is welcome. Family, friends, and company outings are welcomed. Parties of 4 to 8 are recommended; smaller groups are welcome. For private hunts, a minimum of 4 is required.

A $100 deposit is required per day upon confirmation of booking. Balance of payment is due on the day of the trip. Deposits are refundable with a request fourteen (14) days prior to the scheduled booking, or may be transferred to an available date with a request five (5) days prior.

Lodging can be arranged at one of the nearby hotels if needed.

Call for company discounts. Catered fish frys and barbecue available.

Four Seasons Guide Service is a licensed and insured professional outfitter. Hunting trips are not without inherent risk. We spare no effort for a safe trip, but cannot assume responsibility for injuries beyond our control.
