All fishing should be booked in advance. Seven days a week. We suggest a 2–3 day trip, but a 1-day trip is welcome. Family, friends, and company outings are welcomed. Parties of 4 to 8 are recommended; smaller groups are welcome.

A $100 deposit is required per boat, per day upon confirmation of booking. Balance of payment is due on the day of the trip. Deposits are refundable with a request fourteen (14) days prior to the scheduled booking, or may be transferred to an available date with a request five (5) days prior.

Lodging can be arranged at one of the nearby hotels if needed. Charter your trip today to fish one of the best lakes in Texas — and for that matter, the United States.

Call for company discounts. Catered fish frys and barbecue available.

Four Seasons Guide Service is a licensed and insured professional outfitter. Fishing charters are not without inherent risk. We spare no effort for a safe trip, but cannot assume responsibility for injuries beyond our control.
