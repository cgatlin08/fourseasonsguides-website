Frequently asked questions

Helpful details for planning your fishing or hunting trip.

What we provide — Fishing

All fishing equipment (rods, reels, tackle, live bait, etc.) at no extra cost. Licensed and insured guides. Fish until the limit is reached or up to 8 hours.

What we provide — Hunting

Pre-scouted public/private areas, professional guides, blinds, decoys, and professional dogs.

What you should bring — Fishing

Fishing license, wet-weather gear, food and drinks.

What you should bring — Hunting

Waterfowl license, federal duck stamp, Texas or Oklahoma state duck stamp, 10 or 12 gauge gun, steel shot only (2s, 3s, or 4s suggested), camouflage, hip boots or chest waders, and wet/cold weather gear.
